Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and mobile recharge process

To recharge your mobile number, simply enter your number on our platform and click "Continue." You'll be prompted to enter your email address, after which you can browse available plans. Select a plan that suits your needs, proceed to payment using your preferred method (UPI, card, net banking, or digital wallet), and upon successful payment, you'll receive a confirmation.

We support all major mobile operators in the country, including but not limited to Airtel, Jio, Vi (Vodafone Idea), and BSNL. Our platform is continuously updated to include the latest plans and offers from all supported operators.

Most recharges are processed instantly, and you should see the benefits reflected on your mobile number within seconds to a few minutes. In rare cases, it might take up to 30 minutes due to network congestion or technical issues with the operator. If your recharge isn't processed within 30 minutes, please contact our customer support.

We accept a wide range of payment methods for your convenience, including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.), credit/debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to ensure your financial information remains safe.

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. You can reach us through email at support@mobicomm.com, call our toll-free number at 1800-123-4567, or use the live chat feature on our website. We aim to respond to all queries within 2 hours during business hours and within 12 hours during off-hours.
